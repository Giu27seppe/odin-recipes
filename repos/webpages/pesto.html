Pesto

Pesto is a vibrant green, fresh-tasting sauce that originated in Genova, Liguria region, Italy. Itâ€™s traditionally made with a combination of fresh basil, Parmigiano, olive oil, garlic, and pine nuts.

Ingredients

In order to obtain a good Pesto we need to:

  • Basil/Basilico
  • Parmigiano
  • EVO Oil
  • Pine nuts/Pinoli
  • Garlic
  • Lemon

Steps

These are the steps to follow in order to obtain a good Pesto

  1. Step 1: Ingredients combination

    Combine basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor or blender.

  2. Step 2: Blending

    Blend to a smooth paste. Add lemon juice, if desired, and quickly pulse to combine.
